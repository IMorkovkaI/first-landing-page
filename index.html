What will kickboxing training bring you?

Regular kickboxing training can shape your figure, improve your condition and make you feel better overall. If you have problems with excess weight, practical kickboxing will help you to lose weight and reduce body fat. Are you suffering from stress? With kickboxing, you will relax perfectly!

Who is kickboxing for?

The answer is very easy: for everyone! It doesn't matter age or gender, all that matters is desire and determination. Both complete beginners and advanced wrestlers can come to kickboxing courses. Our professional trainers can also tailor training for you according to your physical abilities and prerequisites.

How does fitness kickboxing work?

Fitness kickboxing is a light form of fighting, during which the fighters must not go into full contact and force, the techniques in this discipline are conducted with a light touch. The fighters face each other. Imitation of kickboxing techniques and foresight are key.
